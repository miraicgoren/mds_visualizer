Explore the 3D Perceptual Space

This interactive viewer shows estimated perceptual spaces derived from odd-one-out judgements.

The positions of stimuli are obtained using a 3D multidimensional scaling (MDS) solution, which places stimuli in a three-dimensional space such that distances reflect perceived similarity.

Use the left panel to select the experiment, condition, and cluster, and to select display options and vector fits.

Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, and hover over a stimulus to view its feature values.

Select an experiment, condition, and cluster to explore the estimated perceptual space.
